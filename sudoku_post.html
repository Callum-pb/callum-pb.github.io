Sudoku Solver

Solving Sudoku Puzzles From An Image

I've always been a big fan of Sudoku. However, sometimes I come across one in the newspaper or a book that I cannot solve and do not have the solution to. I've always wanted a way of checking the answers. So I decided to built a program that would do just that.

This started as a simple Colab Notebook that utilised a back-tracking algorithm to solve a Sudoku puzzle given a 2D-array. The next stage is to use CV2 to read an image of an unsolved Sudoku puzzle, parse it to a 2D-array to be solved by the previously used solver.

As you can see, there's still plenty to do. I'll keep updating this notebook and hopefully soon the full program will be available to see here.
